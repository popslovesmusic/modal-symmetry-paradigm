### 3.4 Semantic Curvature (λ)

Bias, framing, or context that warps interpretation.  
Equivalent to GR’s curvature tensor.

### 3.5 Semantic Viscosity (Dignity, D)

Resistance to catastrophic collapse.

# 4\. MSC Axioms

### 4.1 Cohesion Axiom

Semantic objects with high μₛ resist modification.

### 4.2 Curvature Axiom

Semantic mass curves semantic space, influencing neighbor concepts.

### 4.3 Conservation Axiom

Semantic momentum Πₛ is conserved unless acted on by a semantic force.

### 4.4 Dignity Axiom

High viscosity stabilizes high-stress states.  
Low dignity collapses under semantic load.

### 4.5 Causal Horizon Axiom

Influence cannot exceed χₛ.  
Semantic communities can become causally disconnected.

### 4.6 Entropy Axiom

Semantic entropy increases unless active semantic work is performed.

### 4.7 Stress Limit Axiom

If σ \> σ\_critical, the Box fractures (semantic trauma, schism).
